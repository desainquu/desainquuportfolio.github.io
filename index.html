About Me

We work in a team, consisting of various expertise in the field of design. The designs we make are vector designs and mockups using adobe illustration and adobe photoshop as well as electrical and architectural designs using AutoCad and Sketchup.

We have been in the design world for 2 years especially for vector and mockup design and 4 years for electrical and architectural design. We have been active as freelancers for the last 1 year on freelancer sites such as fiverr, shutterstock, and fastwork.
